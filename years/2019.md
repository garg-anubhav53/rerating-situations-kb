# 2019 — Re-Rating Situations

**Thesis of the year:** 2019 was a **broad, liquidity-driven melt-up** — the S&P 500 rose ~+29% (its best
year since 2013) as the Fed pivoted from hiking (Dec 2018) to *cutting* three times, ending quantitative
tightening and re-expanding its balance sheet ("not-QE"). Because the whole index already re-rated, a clean
**≥2x within the calendar year was RARE at the large-cap level** — only a handful of mega-caps (AMD, Lam
Research) doubled. The real ≥2x cohort lived in three legible pockets:

1. **Clinical-catalyst biotech** — the single richest bucket for 10x moves. Left-for-dead micro-caps that
   posted decisive late-stage data re-rated violently: **AXSM** (Axsome, ~10-35x on AXS-05 depression data),
   **CSTL/Constellation** (~10x on CPI-0610 myelofibrosis), **KOD/Kodiak** (~10x on KSI-301 wet-AMD),
   **ARWR/Arrowhead** (~4x, RNAi platform validated by J&J/Amgen licensing), plus 2019-IPO **KRTX/Karuna**
   (~3.5x on KarXT schizophrenia data).
2. **The 2019 growth/IPO + platform-momentum cohort** — the year's hot growth narratives ran hard:
   **ENPH/Enphase** (~5.5x, the marquee re-rating of the year — solar micro-inverter margin turn + IQ8),
   **SE/Sea Ltd** (~4x, Free Fire gaming cash-cow funding Shopee), **ROKU** (~4.4x streaming/ad platform),
   **BYND/Beyond Meat** (IPO'd to a 9x peak then round-tripped to ~3x), **SNAP** (~3x turnaround),
   **TTD/PAYC/SHOP** (SaaS/ad-platform doublers), plus semis (**AMD ~2.5x**, **LRCX ~2.2x**) and solar
   (**SEDG ~2x**).
3. **A screener-only micro/OTC + international tail** — where the surprising, un-narrated movers hide:
   **SOBR** (~30x OTC alcohol-detection nano), **SSI/Stage Stores** (~11x dead-retailer squeeze, bankrupt
   2020), **OESX/Orion Energy** (~5-6x LED-lighting turnaround, no press); and internationally the
   **Adani complex** (ADANIGREEN ~4x, Adani Gas/Transmission), **IRCTC** (~3-4x rail-monopoly IPO),
   **Li Ning** (~3x China-streetwear rebrand), **Wuliangye** (baijiu boom), and European semi-equipment /
   bioprocessing doublers (Aixtron, Besi, Sartorius, Genmab).

**Dominant split:** the biggest, most durable re-rates were **genuine business-change** stories —
clinical de-risking (AXSM/CSTL/KOD), a real margin/product inflection (ENPH), or a validated platform
(SE, ARWR, TTD). The tail was **narrative/flow on tiny floats** (SOBR, SSI, BYND's peak) that round-tripped.
2019's lesson is that in an *already-expensive* index, the ≥2x names are concentrated where a specific
catalyst re-priced a specific asset — not spread across the market as in 2020/2021.
